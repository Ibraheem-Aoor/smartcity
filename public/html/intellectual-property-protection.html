Patent, Trademark, and Copyright Registration
  • Patent Registration: Provide guidance on the types of patents available (invention, utility model, design) and the requirements for obtaining patents in different countries.
  • Trademark Registration: Explain the process of registering trademarks and the importance of protecting brand identity.
  • Copyright Protection: Advise businesses on copyright law and the protection of literary, artistic, and musical works.
Dispute Resolution Mechanisms
