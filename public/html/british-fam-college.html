Welcome To British FAM College

Leading the Region in Facilities and Asset Management Education

Vision: To be amongst the best vocational and professional institutions in the region, providing students/trainees with the skills, knowledge, and experiences in Facilities and Asset Management (FAM) for the Knowledge Edge of the 21st-century economy.

Mission: The BFAMC will provide lifelong educational opportunities and experiences through academic and innovative career preparation, service learning, and multiple program delivery modalities that lead to technical certificates, diplomas, and academic degrees to make development and success possible in a competitive global economy.

Academic Excellence

Our programs are designed to equip students with the latest industry knowledge and hands-on experience, ensuring they excel in the competitive field of Facilities and Asset Management.

Innovative Career Preparation

We focus on innovative career preparation through service learning and multiple program delivery modalities, providing a pathway to success in a global economy.

Lifelong Learning

At BFAMC, we are committed to lifelong learning, offering educational opportunities that lead to technical certificates, diplomas, and academic degrees, empowering students to thrive in the 21st-century economy.

Key Competencies in Facilities and Asset Management

Develop essential skills for managing modern facilities and assets, focusing on strategy, operations, sustainability, and technology.

Project Management

Master the art of planning, executing, and closing projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

Leadership and Strategy

Enhance your ability to lead teams and develop strategic initiatives that align with organizational goals.

Operations and Maintenance

Learn how to efficiently manage day-to-day operations and maintenance of facilities to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Performance and Quality

Implement quality control measures and performance benchmarks to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of your facilities.

Communications

Strengthen your communication skills to effectively manage stakeholder relationships and ensure clear, concise information exchange.

Real Estate

Gain insights into property management, real estate investments, and asset valuation to maximize the value of your facilities.

Risk Management

Develop strategies to identify, assess, and mitigate risks associated with facility management and operations.

Finance and Business

Enhance your understanding of financial principles, budgeting, and business management to support effective decision-making.

Occupancy and Human Factors

Focus on space planning, ergonomics, and user experience to create environments that enhance productivity and well-being.

Facility Information and Technology Management

Leverage technology and data management tools to optimize facility operations, maintenance, and asset management.

Sustainability

Adopt sustainable practices in facility management to reduce environmental impact and promote long-term resource efficiency.
